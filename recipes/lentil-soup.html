Lentil Soup

Lentil soup in a smooth and light manner. With just a few simple ingredients, now you can make this traditional dish at your home, too.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 1 cup of chopped onion
  • 1/2 sliced potato
  • 3/2 tea spoon salt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 spoon butter
  • 1 liter water

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Mix them all with a blender
  3. Put the mixture in a pot
  4. Cook for about 30 minutes
  5. Enjoy, your lentil soup is ready!
